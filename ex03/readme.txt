Program Description:
--------------------
This program extracts and prints each digit of a given number in reverse order, including leading zeros.

Test Cases:
-----------
1. Input: 12345
   Output: 54321
   Explanation: The digits of 12345 are printed in reverse order.

2. Input: 9876
   Output: 6789
   Explanation: The digits of 9876 are printed in reverse order, including leading zeros.

3. Input: 500
   Output: 005
   Explanation: The digits of 500 are printed in reverse order, with leading zeros retained.
